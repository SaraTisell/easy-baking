Have you heard about "Fika"?
In Sweden fika is sainted. Whenever you want to see some one, ask for a fika. At work, it is important to have a fika. Most of the time when we celibrating birthdays, we do not invite to specific birthday parties, we invite to fika!

So what is "fika" you may wonder..
It is usually a cup of coffe or tea togheter with a pice of HEAVEN - Chocolate cake, Carrot cake, Cheesecake, Cookies, with or without chocolate chip, Cupcakes, Muffins or you name it!
